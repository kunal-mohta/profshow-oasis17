Electronic Music Festival Christina Novelli To be
Announced discover more
Christina Novelli is a British DJ vocalist and Songwriter from London. In early 2012, Novelli collaborated with producer and DJ Gareth Emery, on the track 'Concrete Angel'. This song had entered the Billboard magazine at #15 on the 'Dance Music' charts, and currently has well over 30 million views on YouTube. Since then, Novelli has also collaborated with the likes of Dash Berlin and Armin Van Buuren. In December 2013, "Satellite" by Justin Caruso & Dave Audé featuring Christina Novelli, debuted as the #47 Dance Club Song in Billboard magazine.

Indie Night Parvaaz and Ganesh Talkies Date: 1st November
Time: 9:30 PM
Venue: Central Auditorium discover more
This Oasis, 'Indie Night' brings to you two of the coolest bands in the Indian circuit -Parvaaz and Ganesh Talkies.

Parvaaz is a Bangalore-based four-piece rock band formed in 2010. With varied compositions of upbeat, funk, progressive and mellow, slow, songs Parvaaz caters to a wide audience in their performances. Their Indian influence is evident from their Urdu, Hindi and occasionally Kashmiri lyrics. The band recently opened for UK rock-band Alt-J in the Emerge Festival.

Ganesh Talkies is an alternative rock band from Kolkata. Taking inspiration from artists ranging from Damon Albarn to Radiohead to Bappi Lahiri, they classify their music as Alt /Rock /Pop /Reggae /Ska /Bollywood. The band delivers 80’s style heavy synth driven music with a catchy refrain that will occupy a permanent space in your mid nd’s ear. The Ganesh Talkies and Parvaaz are currently playing, at this very moment, in the NH7 Weekender in Shillong as you read this. In a week’s time, they will bring the magic of this adrenaline-driven festival on campus.
